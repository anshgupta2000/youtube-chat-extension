1. Information We Collect

TubeChat AI collects and processes the following data:

  • YouTube video IDs of videos you interact with through the extension
  • Chat history within the extension
  • Feedback you provide on AI responses

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI-powered chat functionality
  • Generate video summaries and insights
  • Improve our service based on your feedback

3. Data Storage and Security

Your chat history is stored locally on your device. Feedback data is sent to our secure servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use anonymized, aggregated data for service improvement.

5. Your Rights

You can clear your chat history at any time by clearing your browser data for this extension.
